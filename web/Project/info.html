Rain Drop Racing

Have you ever been driving in the rain and watched two raindrops fall down the window and tried to guess which raindrop would get to the bottom first? This is the basic idea of this mobile game, but you are the raindrop and you are racing other raindrops. As you collect water while falling, you fall faster. There are powerups to help you and obstacles to avoid, with different maps and levels of difficulty to keep things interesting. The hope is that this game will actually be developed for Apple users by the end of the year. (Android to come later)

The Purpose of this Website

The main purpose of this website was to design the foundation of the database needed to store the data for users, maps, games played, etc. for the RainDrop Racing Game. Although there is no way to actually play the game on this website, there lies the ability to play randomly generated, simulated games. This fulfills the scope of CSE 341 as it allows the creation, inserting, updating and retrieving of data from a database.
